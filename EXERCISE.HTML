Diet Tips

Important Nutrition Guidelines

  • Drink at least 3-4 liters of water throughout the day
  • Include protein in every meal for muscle recovery
  • Choose complex carbs over simple carbs
  • Eat healthy fats like nuts, seeds, and olive oil
  • Include fiber-rich foods for better digestion
  • Limit processed foods and sugar
  • Eat every 3-4 hours to maintain metabolism
  • Portion control is key - use smaller plates

Foods to Include

  • Proteins: Eggs, chicken, fish, lentils, tofu, Greek yogurt
  • Carbs: Brown rice, oats, quinoa, sweet potatoes, whole wheat
  • Fats: Avocado, nuts, seeds, olive oil, coconut oil
  • Vegetables: Spinach, broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, tomatoes
  • Fruits: Berries, apples, bananas, oranges, papaya

Foods to Avoid

  • Sugar and sugary drinks
  • Processed foods and snacks
  • Fried foods and excessive oil
  • White bread and refined flour
  • Alcohol and excessive caffeine
